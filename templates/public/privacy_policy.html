TripMate Fleet Platform

Privacy Policy

TripMate helps drivers, transporters, and fleet admins manage trips, diesel logs, attendance, reports, alerts, and route monitoring.

Data we collect

  • Account details such as username, email, phone number, company name, and vehicle or driver assignments.
  • Trip and attendance data such as odometer readings, trip start and end times, destinations, fuel logs, uploaded photos, and billing records.
  • Location data when trip tracking is enabled for an open trip.
  • Notification tokens for delivery of trip alerts and update alerts.

How we use data

  • To start, monitor, and close trips correctly.
  • To show live trip tracking to authorized admins and transporters.
  • To generate fuel, diesel, salary, and billing reports.
  • To send trip alerts, service reminders, and operational notifications.

Location tracking

Driver location is collected only for fleet trip operations. In the driver app, location tracking may continue while a trip is open so the fleet can monitor the active run, even if the app is minimized. Tracking should stop once the trip is closed.

Sharing

TripMate does not sell personal data. Operational data is shared only with the authorized transporter, assigned fleet staff, and admins who need it to run the service.

Retention

Some operational records such as trip history, diesel logs, attendance, and billing records may be retained where needed for business, compliance, or audit purposes.

Account deletion

You can request account deletion from within the app profile page or through the website deletion page: Account Deletion Request.
